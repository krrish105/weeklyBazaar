Saturday Market

Market organized every week for providing customers with varities of products along with lots of memories!!

Details

Timings:
 3pm to 8pm, Saturday
Address:
 Mangal Chauk, near Aditya Mall

History

This market was first organized on March, 2010. Since then, on every Saturday this market is organized and various people come here to enjoy while buying the necessary items.
This market was organized when two brothers were tired of going to various places for buying basic things, and also in the mall the same things were so expensive. So they contacted various vendors and convinced them to sell their products on discount and at a decided venue on every Saturday.
